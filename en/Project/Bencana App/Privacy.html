Privacy Policy – Bencana Indonesia App

Welcome to Bencana Indonesia. This app is designed to provide up-to-date information related to disasters across Indonesia, such as weather updates, air quality, tsunamis, earthquakes, and other relevant data from trusted institutions.

User privacy is very important to the Developer. Therefore, the Developer is committed to being transparent about how this app collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data during your use of the Bencana Indonesia application.

1. User Consent

By downloading, accessing, or using the Bencana Indonesia app, you are deemed to have read, understood, and agreed to all contents of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with part or all of the terms, you are advised not to use the application.

2. Data Usage

To enhance the user experience, the app may access and process the following data:

  • Location (GPS): Used to display disaster-related information based on your area.
  • Manual Address: If GPS is unavailable, users can manually input location codes through the provided feature.
  • Technical Data: Such as device region, device language, operating system, and usage logs, used for analytics and performance improvements.

Sensitive data like location and address may be processed and transmitted to related government institutions, except for the Ministry of Environment and map service providers. Further explanation is available in section 4.

3. Links to Other Apps or Websites

This app may contain links or buttons that direct to third-party services or websites, including those manually added by users. The Developer has no control over the content or privacy policies of such third-party services.

By accessing these links, you acknowledge that your data may be collected by third parties, and any risks arising are your sole responsibility as the user.

4. Third-Party Services

Data retrieval and processing in this app are conducted through third-party services. The app will connect directly to these services and only transmit necessary data as required.

The third-party services used in this app include:

  • Indonesian Agency for Meteorology, Climatology, and Geophysics (BMKG)
  • Geospatial Information Agency (BIG)
  • Ministry of Environment (Kemenlh)
  • Ministry of Communication and Digital (Komdigi)

For map display, the app uses OpenStreetMap by default. If users choose to use a different map provider, it is recommended to review the terms and privacy policies of each service beforehand.

The Developer does not have control over how third-party services manage and protect user data. Therefore, users are strongly encouraged to read and understand each provider’s privacy policy through their official websites before using such services.

5. Emergency Calls

This app provides a feature to facilitate users in making emergency calls. Calls will be made through the selected app or phone service.

The Developer has no control over third-party services used for making calls and is not responsible for any legal consequences or other risks that may arise from using this feature.
